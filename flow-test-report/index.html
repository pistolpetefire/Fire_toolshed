Hydrant flow test

Flow at residual may be typed directly. Editing pitot pressure, outlet diameter, coefficient, Monster Pitot Chart, or extra flowing hydrants recalculates the flow using Q = 29.83 x C x d^2 x sqrt(P), rounded to the nearest 1 gpm. When Monster Pitot Chart is included, flow uses the chart's 0.9 coefficient basis rounded to the nearest 5 gpm. Extra flowing hydrants multiply the single-hydrant flow by 2 or 3. Building allowances

18 psi is a conservative screening allowance for uncalculated system friction, including lead-in/riser effects and estimated piping loss to the remote design area, but excluding BFP impact. It is meant to represent a Hangar with AF Sundown Policy. A light hazard building could be lowered toward 10 psi.

Backflow preventer

Scope flags

  • Tracks whether NFPA 13, UFC 3-600-01, and Air Force Sundown criteria apply to the scenario.
  • When UFC is selected, sprinkler demand options use UFC 3-600-01 Table 9-3.
  • When Air Force Sundown is selected, sprinkler demand uses 0.20 gpm/sf over 5,000 sf with minimum K 11.2 unless another selected special criterion is greater.
  • Backflow preventer loss adds 8 psi for double check or 12 psi for reduced pressure when required.
  • Handles density-area sprinkler estimates for light, ordinary, and extra hazard occupancies.
  • Does not size storage, ESFR, CMSA, dry system delivery, standpipe demand, private fire mains, or pump selection curves.
  • Add backflow, meter, underground, riser, main, and branchline losses into other system losses if no detailed calc exists.
